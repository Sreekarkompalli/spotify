Any questions?

How do I create a playlist?

Playlists are a great way to save collections of music, either for your own listening or to share.

To create one:

  1. Tap Your Library.
  2. Tap CREATE.
  3. Give your playlist a name.
  4. Start adding songs (and we’ll help you along).

How do I activate Data Saver mode?

  1. Tap Home.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Tap Data Saver.
  4. Switch on Data Saver.

Is it only possible to shuffle play music?

Any playlist with the Shuffle icon can be listened to on Shuffle.

Playlist without the Shuffle icon are available to Pick and Play. These are playlists you can play in any order and skip tracks as much as you like.

Where can I find Podcasts?

Tap Search, then look for Podcasts under Genres & Moods.
